Nebraska The Good Cheap Life

Nebraska's one of happiest states in the Union. It boasts having one of the highest Well-Being Index scores, and is home to the happiest city in America.

On top of all that, it's not expensive to live here.

Jobs in Nebraska

The Cornhusker State has the lowest unemployment of the nation. The only other states that come close are North and South Dakota.

Living in Nebraska

Great, almost everyone who wants to work has a job. Even better, the cost of living is low. Nebraska's tied with Kansas and Alabama as the 8th cheapest state to live in.

Nebraskan Income

While it's not the richest state in America, Nebraska's median income is above the U.S. average. That's not too bad, considering the low cost of living.
